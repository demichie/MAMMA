MAMMA - Finite volume solver

MAMMA is a FORTRAN90 code designed to solved a conservative model for compressible two-phase flow by finite volume methods. The model is one-dimensional with different phase velocities and pressures but a single temperature for the two phases.
The finite volume solver is based on a semidiscrete central scheme and it is not tied on the specific eigenstructure of the model.

  • 1D transient flow
